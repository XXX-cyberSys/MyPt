About command 'my client'

This command print your login and password in console where you start this programm
Exemple: 
If you start this programm in Windows PowerShell - your info in print in PowerShel
If you copy my code and compiled there - info is print in Geany-console or VSCode-comsole or when you starting

Printing exemple:


    Enter your command: my client

    --- Account Data ---
    login: Masha
    password: 12345

    Enter your command: _|


!!!WARING!!! You must create account before usind this command
If you didn't create client-account or remove account-folder, your info look like:


    Enter your command: my client

    --- Account Data ---
    Login: [Not Found]
    Password: [Not Found]

    Enter your command: _|
